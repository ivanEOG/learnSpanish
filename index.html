The verb to be. (ser/estar)

How to use the verb to be in spanish

In spanish we separate the verb to be in two diferent verbs (ser/estar). The verb ser means "being something" or "being like something"

Example:

Yo soy inteligente

I am intelligent

The verb estar refers to a physical state of "being somewhere". Estar also indicates temporary states
